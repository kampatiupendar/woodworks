Manufacturing Unit

Upendar Wood works, where craftsmanship meets creativity. With over 20 years of experience in the furniture industry, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, handcrafted furniture made using premium materials like teak wood, transforming your space into a true reflection of your style.


Every piece we create—from luxurious sofas to custom beds and stylish dining sets—is built with precision, passion, and the finest materials, including durable teak wood. Whether you're furnishing your dream home or enhancing your workspace, our mission is to provide furniture solutions that combine aesthetics with functionality.


At Upendar Wood works, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with each client to understand their vision and deliver results that exceed expectations. Join our growing family of happy clients and experience the timeless beauty of finely crafted teak wood furniture.
